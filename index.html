My Career so Far...

Hi, I'm Branka, my work experience so far includes many different positions, but I got a chance in the business part and managed to learn it, so for the last +7 years I've been working as an accountant. Although the desire for art and design has always been smoldering in me, so I decided to follow my instinct and try to learn what has always been my passion - design! Studying at the Academy of UX/UI Design was an encouragement and a challenge for me, I understand that perfect design involves not only visual aesthetics, but more importantly, interface that works well.
This requires a deep understanding of human behavior and psychology - the outcome of which greatly affects the overall product experience, regardless of platform.
